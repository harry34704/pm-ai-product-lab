# Netflix Product Teardown

## Problem Being Solved

Netflix helps users discover and consume premium video entertainment without the friction of linear programming schedules.

## Target Users

- Global consumers looking for on-demand entertainment
- Households with varied viewing preferences
- Mobile-first viewers who want offline and cross-device access

## Business Model

Netflix primarily monetizes through subscription revenue, with an additional ad-supported tier broadening price coverage.

## Growth Strategy

- Localized content investment
- Strong recommendation loops
- Cross-device ubiquity
- Brand equity around exclusive originals

## Product Architecture Assumptions

- Large-scale recommendation systems
- Streaming optimization infrastructure
- Content metadata pipelines
- Payments, identity, and entitlement services

## Improvement Ideas

- Better collaborative watch planning for households
- Transparent recommendation controls for power users
- More proactive churn-prevention messaging tied to dormant accounts
