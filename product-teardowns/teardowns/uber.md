# Uber Product Teardown

## Problem Being Solved

Uber reduces the friction of arranging reliable transportation and local delivery in real time.

## Target Users

- Riders needing fast urban transport
- Drivers seeking flexible income
- Merchants and consumers using delivery services

## Business Model

Uber monetizes through take rates on rides and deliveries, plus enterprise mobility products and membership programs.

## Growth Strategy

- Geographic density
- Two-sided marketplace liquidity
- Pricing incentives and promotions
- Expansion into adjacent logistics behaviors

## Product Architecture Assumptions

- Dispatch and routing systems
- Dynamic pricing infrastructure
- Fraud and trust systems
- Driver, rider, and merchant identity layers

## Improvement Ideas

- Better rider transparency around surge drivers
- More flexible commute bundles for repeat riders
- Deeper post-trip issue prevention before support tickets occur
