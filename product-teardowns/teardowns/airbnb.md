# Airbnb Product Teardown

## Problem Being Solved

Airbnb helps travelers find unique lodging while enabling hosts to monetize underused space.

## Target Users

- Leisure and group travelers
- Hosts managing one or multiple properties
- Long-stay and remote-work travelers

## Business Model

Airbnb monetizes through booking fees across guests and hosts, with incremental monetization from host tools and services.

## Growth Strategy

- Supply expansion through host acquisition
- Trust-building through reviews and guarantees
- Category merchandising for discovery
- International expansion and localization

## Product Architecture Assumptions

- Search and ranking systems for listings
- Host calendar and pricing infrastructure
- Payments, identity, and trust & safety services
- Messaging, review, and policy enforcement systems

## Improvement Ideas

- Smarter price-quality explanations during booking
- Better long-stay workflows for repeat business travelers
- Host productivity tools for common operational tasks
