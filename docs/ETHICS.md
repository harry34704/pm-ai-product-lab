# Ethics

MockRoom AI is designed for interview practice only.

## Allowed Use

- mock interviews
- rehearsal
- structured preparation
- visible coaching during practice sessions
- transcript review with explicit consent

## Disallowed Use

- real-time covert assistance during actual interviews
- hidden overlays or teleprompters
- invisible or disguised windows
- click-through windows
- stealth microphone capture
- platform detection evasion
- deceptive or fabricated resume claims

## Product Guardrails

- Coaching is displayed in a clearly labeled `Practice Mode` panel.
- Microphone and transcript features require explicit consent.
- Suggestions must stay evidence-based and truthful.
- Resume and job analysis may infer emphasis areas, but it may not invent experience.
